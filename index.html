Privacy Policy

Merlin: Multi-LLM & AI Macro Chrome Extension

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

Compliance Note: This policy is designed to comply with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

1. Introduction

"Merlin: Multi-LLM & AI Macro" (the "Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy covers how we collect, use, store, and share your data when you use the Extension.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Personally Identifiable Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your Email Address and Google Profile Identifier.
  • Subscription and Payment Data: If you purchase a donation or subscription, we process your Transaction ID and Subscription Status via our payment provider.
  • Usage Configuration: We store your Custom Macro steps, Layout preferences, and Active bot selections.
  • Authentication Tokens: Temporary tokens are stored to keep you logged into the service.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: Providing secure login via Google OAuth 2.0.
  • Service Delivery: Syncing your custom bots and macro settings across devices (if logged in).
  • Membership Management: Verifying your "Coffee" or "Pro" status to unlock features.
  • Performance Monitoring: Basic error logs (non-identifiable) to maintain service stability.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We only share your data with the following essential service providers:

  • Google LLC: Provides authentication services (OAuth).
  • Supabase (Backend/Database): Stores your profile ID, email, and macro settings securely.
  • Lemon Squeezy (Payments): Processes donations and subscriptions. They collect your billing information separately; we only receive non-sensitive transaction confirmation.
  • AI Providers (OpenAI, Google, Anthropic): When you send a prompt, your text is transmitted directly to their websites through an iframe. We do not intercept, view, or store the content of your conversations.

Important: We do not sell your personal data to any third parties or marketing agencies.

5. Google User Data Policy (Limited Use)

Merlin's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We do not use your Google data for serving advertisements or for any purposes other than providing and improving the Extension's features.

6. Data Security and Retention

  • Encryption: All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored with industry-standard encryption at rest.
  • Retention: We retain your profile and macro data as long as your account is active. You can request immediate deletion of all your data at any time.
  • Local Data: All non-synced settings are stored locally in your browser (LocalStorage) and can be cleared by you at any time.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Access & Export: You can view your settings and macros within the Extension UI.
  • Deletion: You can logout and uninstall the Extension. To delete your cloud account, please contact us.
  • Opt-out: You can choose not to sign in with Google, though this will limit features to local storage only.
