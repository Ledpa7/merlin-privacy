Privacy Policy

Merlin: Multi-LLM & AI Macro Chrome Extension

Effective Date: January 29, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how "Merlin: Multi-LLM & AI Macro" (the "Extension") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and profile picture. This is used solely for authentication and account management.
  • Local Storage Data: Your custom macros and preferences are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly sync it.

We do NOT collect:

  • Your search queries or prompts
  • Your conversations with AI services
  • Your browsing history
  • Any personal data beyond what is listed above

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: To verify your identity when you sign in with Google.
  • Account Management: To identify your account for premium features (if applicable).
  • Service Improvement: We may use anonymized, aggregated data to improve the Extension.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Authentication Data: Stored securely in Supabase (our authentication provider) with industry-standard encryption.
  • Local Data: Stored in your browser's local storage and Chrome sync storage.
  • Security: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption.

5. Third-Party Services

The Extension interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Google Identity Services: For Google Sign-In authentication.
  • Supabase: For secure user authentication and account management.
  • AI Services (Gemini, ChatGPT, Claude): Your prompts are sent directly to these services through their official websites. We do not intercept or store this data.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is only shared with the service providers listed above, solely for the purpose of providing the Extension's functionality.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw Consent: Sign out of the Extension at any time to stop data collection.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below.

8. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account or request deletion.
  • Local Data: Remains on your device until you uninstall the Extension or clear your browser data.

9. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the Extension or our website. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
